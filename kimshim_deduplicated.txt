From the bottom of my heart,<b>Meaning:</b> With sincere and deep feeling. <br><br> <b>Origin:</b> The deepest part of the heart is seen as the most honest. <br><br> <b>Example:</b> I want to thank you from the bottom of my heart for your help.
Full of hot air,<b>Meaning:</b> Talking a lot without saying anything important or true. <br><br> <b>Origin:</b> Likening a person's speech to the useless steam or air in a balloon. <br><br> <b>Example:</b> Don't listen to him; he’s just full of hot air.
Green light,<b>Meaning:</b> Permission to start a project or activity. <br><br> <b>Origin:</b> From traffic signals where a green light means you can proceed. <br><br> <b>Example:</b> The boss finally gave us the green light to start the new campaign.
Haste makes waste,"<b>Meaning:</b> If you do something too quickly, you will make mistakes and waste time or resources. <br><br> <b>Origin:</b> A proverb from the 14th century. <br><br> <b>Example:</b> Slow down and check your work; haste makes waste."
Have a bone to pick with someone,<b>Meaning:</b> To have a complaint or grievance that you want to discuss. <br><br> <b>Origin:</b> From a dog chewing on a bone until every scrap of meat is gone. <br><br> <b>Example:</b> I have a bone to pick with you about the way you spoke to me yesterday.
Hit the jackpot,"<b>Meaning:</b> To have a sudden, great success or to win a lot of money. <br><br> <b>Origin:</b> From slot machines or poker. <br><br> <b>Example:</b> She really hit the jackpot when she found that old painting at the garage sale."
